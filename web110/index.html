About me....

I moved to Seattle, WA on April 24, 2019 from Texas with my partner. For the past 4 years or so, I have worked in the Insurance industry as a Claims Representative/Adjuster in Texas. I figured it was going to be an easy transition for me in Seattle and I would have no problem finding a job in the Insurance industry; however, that was not the case. I did not want to just take any job when I worked so hard in Texas to get where I was, especially income wise. And, so this began to weigh on me. So, one evening, my parter and I talked and thought perhaps this is the best time for a career change and think about what I would like to do for the second half of my life.

I wanted to study something that I was going to enjoy doing, that was the most important. Secondly, I wanted to study something that was in high demand in the employment world. So, I enrolled into Seattle Central College in the Fall Quarter of 2019 and decided to study Web Development. Now, I have NEVER programmed or coded in my life! So, this is very intimidating for me because my knowledge of computers is that of an average every day user. I know how to start and shut off a computer, do google searches, and check my emails. That's it! My plan is to finish up the certificate program and then go after the Associate's program. After that, I hope to find work with a major corporation in the Seattle or Greater Seattle area as an entry-level or Jr. Web Developer.
